E-leKtric

   E-leKtric is a system to transfer power through an air gap. Power is fed to an oscillator that vibrates at a frequency over a distance. The receiver is a grid of billions of nano piezo membrane stacks. The oscillations from the power from power grid are sent through the gap to the stacks and vibrates and we convert this to mechanical energy, piezo electric energy and thermal from the membranes heating up if cooled properly. Billions of stacks equals billions of wires and we need to get rid of that so take another grid of stacks of larger diameter and place it close by almost touching and step down the number of stacks with bigger diameter, then step down again bigger diameter stacks until we have smaller amounts for wiring convenience. This wire less wire system will send signals through a gap using Acoustics and piezoelectricity hartvested from the vibrations picked up by membrane stacks.
