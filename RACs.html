RAC Processor Grid

Similar to band gaps in chemisty connecting each node to its neighbor in all directions. If two are identical the two nodes are entangled information and we can get inter dimensional travel through the nodes faster than light. The RACs, short for Tesseract, are light patterns that are shone through various slits and grating that changes and oscillates. The oscillation is recorded and analyzed frame by frame then the intensity of the light is converted to binary and repeated. This is one node. Possibly interconnected with another if identical. The incoming binary is read vertically and diagonally in blocks of words by 16 lines deep. 16 by 16 bits.
