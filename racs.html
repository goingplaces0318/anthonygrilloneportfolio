trEz

   TrEz are springy structures resembling trees with leaves that catch the wind by vibrating the scoop shape of the leaf when wind that turn with the wind. The geometry of the leaf is similar to that of sunbathing mirror. A scoop of angles that will flutter in the wind and when wind changes direction the scoop makes sure the leaf turns with it. The leafs surface is a grid of perforations with lenses at opening followed by piezoelectric membrane stacks on nano scale that generate power from four free sources. Photovoltaic, wind, thermal and piezoelectric. All wiring housed in trunk. And then thhe trunk is hundreds if not thousands of stacked membranes and the trunk is on sensitive springs so the whole unit sways and picks up the vibrations from the tectonic plates set to tune to low resonance.
